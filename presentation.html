How Each Stage Works

Stage 1: Cleaning

50-row corrupted dataset → up to 15 sequential fix actions

6 Operations
fill_null fix_type remove_duplicate standardize trim correct_typo
Corruptions (15% rate)
Nulls · Type mismatches · Typos · Duplicates · Whitespace
REWARD
0.50 × DQafter + 0.50 × min(Δ × 5, 1.0)
DQ SCORE
0.40 × completeness + 0.35 × consistency + 0.25 × uniqueness

Ends: DQ > 0.95 or step ≥ 15

Stage 2: Enrichment

Clean dataset → add derived fields from 20 domain sources

5 sources per domain (20 total)
HR salary_band, tenure_risk, flight_risk_score...
Sales deal_size_category, velocity_score, win_prob...
PM schedule_risk, burndown_rate, delay_prob...
IT Ops sla_compliance, mttr_band, severity_score...
REWARD
coverage = fields_added / total_available

Ends: coverage > 0.80 or step ≥ 12

Stage 3: Answering

Enriched data + persona + question → cited analytical answer

3 Personas
Executive ROI, revenue, trend, margin, portfolio
Manager team, performance, bottleneck, SLA
IC my, next step, deadline, assigned
REWARD
0.30 × faithfulness + 0.70 × persona_alignment
Faithfulness: cited column validity + sample value refs
Persona: keyword hits + formality + anti-keyword penalty

Single-step episode (1 action)

"The challenge isn't any single step — it's that the model must learn 3 fundamentally different skills: diagnostic reasoning, knowledge application, and communication adaptation."

Multi-Signal Reward

step_reward = max(quality_delta − 0.02, −0.15)
QUALITY GATE BONUS

FINAL REWARD
0.45 × quality + 0.30 × gate_pass + 0.25 × op_coverage

6 Quality Gates

1 No unresolved missing values
2 No duplicate rows
3 Data consistency (columns + target preserved)
4 Row retention ≥ 85%
5 Dtype alignment (all expected types match)
6 Stability probe (3-fold CV std ≤ 0.15)
