# OpenEnv Hackathon Rules & Judging Criteria

## Submission Rules
- Open Source: Repository must be public
- New Work Only: All projects started from scratch during the hackathon
- Team Size: Up to 3 members
- Must use OpenEnv (stable release 0.2.1) deployed on HF Spaces
- Must show a minimal training script for your environment using Unsloth or HF TRL in Colab
- Must upload a one minute demo video to YouTube

## Judging Criteria
- **Environment Innovation (40%)** - Is the environment novel, creative, or challenging? Does it meaningfully test the agent's behavior?
- **Storytelling (30%)** - Does the team clearly explain the problem, environment, and agent behavior? Is the demo engaging and easy to follow?
- **Training Script Showing Improvement in Rewards (20%)** - Does the demo provide observable evidence of training progress (reward curves, metrics, or before/after behavior)?
- **Reward and Training Pipeline Setup (10%)** - Is the reward logic coherent, and does the pipeline produce meaningful improvement in the agent's inference?
